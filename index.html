Who I Am

About Ashley

I'm Ashley Childs — a self-taught videographer, photographer, and content strategist with 15 years of hands-on experience transforming real moments into powerful, scroll-stopping content. Based in Dallas, TX, I've worked across entertainment, nightlife, sports, automotive, culinary arts, and live events.

What separates me from the rest isn't just the ability to shoot beautifully — it's knowing how to make content perform. I understand platforms, trends, timing, and the psychology behind what makes people stop and engage. My West Indian Labor Day parade coverage generated over 100,000 combined TikTok views and continues to trend organically every Labor Day weekend — proof that great content has a long shelf life.

Whether I'm producing monthly reels for a nightlife brand, photographing vehicle inventory for DFW dealerships, or building a sports team's social presence from zero, I bring the same energy: creative vision backed by strategic execution.
